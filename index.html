About Me

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer passionate about creating intelligent, energy-efficient solutions that power the devices we use every day. Currently driving innovation in Lahore, Pakistan, where I architect firmware for ARM Cortex-based systems and bring complex embedded projects to life.

My journey has taken me through exciting projects, including secure cloud-connected devices, safety systems for industrial workers, and low-power wireless technologies. I love working at the intersection of hardware and software, solving real-world problems with elegant engineering solutions.

Whether it's making a device last months on a single battery, ensuring data stays secure, or building systems that respond in real-time, I bring a combination of technical expertise and practical problem-solving to every project. My goal is always to write code that's not just functional, but reliable and maintainable for the long term.

When I'm not working on firmware or troubleshooting hardware, you'll find me exploring new technologies, learning from the maker community, and staying curious about what's possible in the world of embedded systems.
